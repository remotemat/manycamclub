About Us

Matching Service

We match you with other teachers to bring you ManyCam Subscriptions at the lowest of prices. Just join the waiting list, and wait for your subscription to reach your current ManyCam account.

Join the waiting list!

Once you join the waiting list you will be matched with other teachers seeking the same subscription as yourself. The amount of teachers needed for a list to complete depends on the subscription you choose.

Support

For the life of you subsciption purchased through the ManyCamClub you will recieve our support. Should anything unforseen arise we will be there to help sole the issue

Money Back Guarantee

Currently lists are completing every 3 - 5 days, but if the wait becomes to long, you can cancel your position on the list at anytime. Your payment is made secure through Paypal.

How the ManyCamClub works:

  • Choose your subscription below and join the waiting list.
  • Make your secure payment through Paypal.
  • Sit back, relax and wait for your list to have enough teachers.
  • Once your list has enough teachers, you will be notified by email and the subscription will be sent to your ManyCam account.
  • Activate your subscription in the ManyCam Application and happy teaching!.
